Immunization Instructions

Please provide your child’s immunization records to the school on or before your child’s first day in our school . You can ask your doctor's office to fax the immunization records to the school fax number: 425-882-1833 or you can get the immunization record from the doctor's office in the state's CIS form found in the next page and sign the form.

We have heard from few popular pediatric centers (e.g. Allegro Pediatrics) that they might no longer fax immunization records directly to third parties. In that case, please ask the office to email it to you. You can then forward it to us. Alternatively, you can use self service method to fetch the CIS form yourself at www.myir.net. It will require one time registration and authentication.

If you have an exemption due to medical or religious reasons. Please let us know and we will help provide the proper form to fill out.
